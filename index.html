About

SherlockCode, Inc. is an information technology software service platform that investigates and verifies whether your work has been plagiarized. We have developed an incredibly efficient machine learning algorithm that utilizes both String Matching and Natural Language Processing to increase the verification rate.

Originality Verification

A common situation faced by today's modern creators is to constantly verify the originality of any work. Our system thoroughly interogates your work and will provide a detailed report of any potentially stolen material.

Tracking Content Misuse

Making sure your material is not being misused on the internet is stressful and unneccesary. SherlockCode not only returns one accurate result, but rather produces a report showing every available result.

Deep Source Analysis

Our systems are able to reduced the field of candidates down to a single document. If you are suspecting several different parties, we provide the tools to decisively confirm those suspicions and find the culprit.
